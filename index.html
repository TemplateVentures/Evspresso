    What kind of Coffee we serve for you

  • A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk foam.

  • Caffè Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water.

  • Espresso is one of the most popular coffee-brewing methods, of Italian origin.

  • Caffè macchiato, sometimes called espresso macchiato, is an espresso coffee drink with a small amount of milk, usually foamed.

  • A caffè mocha, also called mocaccino, is a chocolate-flavoured warm beverage that is a variant of a caffè latte.

  • Caffè latte is a coffee drink of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk.

  • Piccolo latte is a fresh roasted coffee that's comprised of a single shot of ristretto that's served in a demitasse glass

  • Ristretto coffee is made with the same amount of ground coffee, but extracted with a finer grind using half as much water.

  • An affogato, more traditionally known as "affogato al caffè", is an Italian coffee-based dessert.
